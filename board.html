The SKI HERE pilot project is guided by a board committed to making Utah's mountains accessible to every Utah family.

The SKI HERE board is leading the effort to establish Utah's first public ski resort — a state-owned skiing initiative built to restore affordable access to the Wasatch for Utah residents. Our mission is to make Ski Utah something every family can actually participate in, not just watch from the valley.
